The objectives of the study are as follows:

1. To develop a system that will store and manage criminal records in a centralized location. This will be achieved through the use of one database with relation of tables.
2. To identify major crime prone areas in Nairobi through cases reported and information stored in the database (automatic generation of reports).
3. To ensure that only authorized personnel have access to the police sensitive information. This will be achieved through the use of login credentials provided by the system admin to the specified people.
