The study will focus mainly on the O.B capturing all details concerning criminal activities in
Nairobi County, Kenya. The users of the system will be the police, investigating officers,
in charge officers and administrators from the different security organs who have all privileges
to manipulate data in the system. The data contents include the type of crime, time of the day,
identity of the victim and suspects, and the location of the committed crime.
